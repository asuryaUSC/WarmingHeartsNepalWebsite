Battling the Invisible Enemy: Inside a COVID Isolation Center!

Ensuring Accessible and Comprehensive Care for Patients in Kathmandu

In the heart of Kathmandu, Warming Hearts Nepal has established a dedicated treatment facility at our isolation center, catering to COVID patients with mild and severe cases. Located in Mid Baneshwor (Ratna Rajya School), Ward No. 10, our facility offers comprehensive medical care, including oxygen support for patients with varying levels of oxygen intake demands. With a commitment to inclusivity and compassionate care, we strive to ensure that no one is left behind in the fight against COVID-19, regardless of their financial status.

Comprehensive Treatment for COVID Patients

At our treatment facility, we provide a range of medical services to address the needs of COVID patients. Our team of specialized doctors and 24-hour nursing staff work diligently to monitor and care for patients, from those with mild symptoms to those requiring oxygen support. We prioritize the well-being of each individual and provide necessary referrals to hospitals for intensive care when required. Currently, our facility has the capacity to treat up to 50 patients, with the potential for expansion.

Accessible Care for All

Warming Hearts Nepal is dedicated to bridging the gap in access to COVID treatment, especially for those who cannot afford hospital visits or face limited availability of beds. We do not discriminate based on financial status, recognizing that every individual deserves equal access to adequate care. Our primary objective is to ensure that no one is left without proper treatment due to their financial constraints. By offering comprehensive care at our treatment facility, we strive to serve the community and provide relief during these challenging times.

Caring for the Community

Our treatment facility serves as a beacon of hope for individuals battling COVID-19 in Kathmandu. We understand the overwhelming pressure on hospitals and the need for alternative options to accommodate the increasing number of cases. Through our facility, we alleviate the strain on healthcare resources while ensuring that patients receive the care they require. Our commitment to comprehensive medical facilities and compassionate support helps instill confidence and a sense of security in patients and their families.

A Unified Effort

The success of our treatment facility is made possible by the collective effort of our dedicated team, supporters, and the community. We extend our deepest gratitude to the healthcare professionals who tirelessly provide their expertise and care to patients. We also acknowledge the generosity and support of individuals and organizations that have contributed to the establishment and ongoing operation of the facility. It is through collaboration and unity that we can overcome the challenges posed by the pandemic and provide the necessary care for those in need.

Warming Hearts Nepal's COVID treatment facility stands as a testament to our commitment to providing accessible and comprehensive care for COVID patients in Kathmandu. Regardless of financial status, we ensure that every patient receives the treatment they require, including oxygen support when needed. By alleviating the burden on hospitals and embracing a compassionate approach, we strive to make a positive impact in the lives of individuals and the community at large. Together, let us continue to stand strong and support one another in the face of this global health crisis.
