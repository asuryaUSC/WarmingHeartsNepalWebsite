Tiny Victories: Embracing Small Steps Every Day!

Providing Nutrition Baskets to Children at Kanti Children's Hospital Oncology

The children at Kanti Children's Hospital Oncology have stories that tug at the heartstrings, but their unwavering courage serves as a beacon of hope. Warming Hearts Nepal deeply empathizes with these young cancer patients, who endure unimaginable pain. Childhood cancer is a curable condition worldwide, but in Nepal, malnutrition poses a significant challenge to survival. We have taken it upon ourselves to change this narrative through our Nutrition Basket program, providing essential food supplements to these children twice a month. We firmly believe that good food plays a vital role in making medicine more effective.

Empowering through Nutrition/h3>

At Warming Hearts Nepal, we are committed to ensuring that every child undergoing cancer treatment receives the nutrition they need to fight the disease and return to the playground. We understand that severe malnutrition can hinder their chances of survival. Our Nutrition Basket program includes the provision of food supplements such as F-100 and F-75, specially designed to treat severe malnutrition. By addressing this critical aspect of their care, we aim to enhance their overall well-being and improve their chances of successful treatment outcomes.

The Power of Good Food

We firmly believe that good food acts as a catalyst for effective medical treatment. By providing nutritious food parcels to the children at Kanti Children's Hospital Oncology, we empower them in their fight against cancer. Each carefully curated Nutrition Basket delivers the essential nutrients they need to strengthen their bodies and boost their immune systems. This support is a testament to our unwavering dedication to these young warriors and our belief that proper nourishment is an integral part of their healing journey.

Gratitude for Humanitarian Support

We are immensely grateful to a compassionate and generous couple who funded the purchase and special delivery of these highly nutritious Food Parcels to 17 children on June 22, 2022. Their act of kindness has made a profound impact on the lives of these young patients, demonstrating that even the smallest gestures can have far-reaching effects. We extend our heartfelt gratitude to them, as well as to the families of our patients and the broader community of supporters who have stood by us throughout this journey.

Recognition and Awards

Warming Hearts Nepal's efforts in supporting childhood cancer patients have garnered recognition and accolades. Alongside the love and affection received from our patients and their families, our organization has been honored with awards such as "The Extra Individual Award" and "The Outstanding Woman Award." These accolades reflect the collective effort and dedication of our team and reaffirm our commitment to making a difference in the lives of these courageous children.

Warming Hearts Nepal is dedicated to nourishing hope in the lives of childhood cancer patients. Through our Nutrition Basket program, we provide essential food supplements to children at Kanti Children's Hospital Oncology, empowering them in their fight against cancer. We are deeply grateful for the support of our donors, supporters, and the wider community in helping us bring comfort and healing to these young warriors. With love, compassion, and good nutrition, we strive to transform their stories of pain into stories of triumph. Together, let us stand united in the belief that every child deserves a chance to heal, grow, and reclaim their smiles.
